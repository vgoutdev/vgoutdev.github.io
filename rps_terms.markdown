## 1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy governs the practices of **VGdeveloper** ("Developer," "We," "Us") regarding the **Rock Paper Scissors Pro** mobile application ("Application" or "Game").

## 2. Information We Collect

**We do not collect any personal or non-personal data from users of this Application.**

The Game is designed as a simple, offline experience. It does not require user accounts, access to sensitive permissions, or an internet connection to function.

* **NO Personal Information (PII):** We do not collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, location, contacts, photos, or any other personally identifiable information.
* **NO Non-Personal Data:** We do not use third-party analytics services (like Google Analytics or Firebase) and do not track usage data, game statistics, or device identifiers (like the Google Advertising ID). All data related to the game's progress (e.g., scores, local settings) is stored exclusively on your device and is not transmitted to us or any third party.
