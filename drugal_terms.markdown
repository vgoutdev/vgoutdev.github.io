# Privacy Policy – Drugal

Last updated: 2026-03-05

Drugal ("we", "our", "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what data is processed when you use the app.

## Data we process

### Camera access
Drugal requests camera permission (`android.permission.CAMERA`) only to:
- scan barcodes,
- capture receipt images,
- run on-device OCR (text recognition).

Camera data is processed on-device for these features. We do not sell camera data.

### Account and cloud data
If you sign in (for example with Google/Firebase Auth), authentication identifiers may be processed to provide account features.
If cloud sync/storage features are enabled, your app content is stored in your project backend (Firebase services used by the app).

### Notifications
If enabled by you, the app may use notifications for reminders.

## How we use data
We use data only to provide core app functionality (inventory/scan/OCR/auth/sync/reminders), improve reliability, and ensure security.

## Data sharing
We do not sell personal data.
Data may be processed by service providers used by the app (e.g., Firebase/Google services) strictly to operate the app.

## Data retention
Data is retained only as long as needed for app functionality and legal/security requirements, or until you delete your account/data where applicable.

## Your choices
- You can deny camera permission, but scan/OCR features will not work.
- You can uninstall the app at any time.
- Where applicable, you can request deletion of your account/data.

## Children
The app is not intended for children under the age required by local law without parental consent.

## Security
We apply reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect data.

## Contact
For privacy questions, contact: **vgoutdev@gmail.com**

## Changes to this policy
We may update this policy. Material changes will be reflected by updating the date above.
